2026
MVP & validation cohort

The live PWA at posture-angle-detect.emergent.host. Recruit 50 patients across two German university rehab clinics for the first clinical-validation cohort — published data is the prerequisite for everything that follows.

Late 2026
CE Class I registration (EU MDR)

What it is: the lowest medical-device risk class in the EU. What it requires: a self-declaration of conformity, technical documentation, post-market surveillance plan, and a Notified Body for compliance review. What it unlocks: we can legally market HorusGait as a medical device across the EU.

2027
Enterprise tier + LATAM pilot

Enterprise tier: B2B SaaS for clinics & rehab hospitals (€199–299/seat) with EHR-ready exports and multi-patient dashboards. LATAM pilot: first commercial deployment outside the EU, riding Horusfit’s existing distribution.

2028
DiGA submission & listing

What DiGA is: Germany’s reimbursement pathway for digital health apps (Digitale Gesundheitsanwendung). What it does: once listed in the BfArM directory, statutory health insurers (GKV — ~73M lives) pay for the app when a physician prescribes it. The economics: ~€300 per patient per quarter, paid directly.

2029+
Scale & data licensing

Scale: LATAM regional hubs and expansion across the wider EU. Data licensing: by Year 4 the longitudinal gait dataset becomes the moat — anonymised aggregates licensed to pharma R&D, prosthetics OEMs, and academic researchers.

Pilot & distribution access

Connections to physiotherapy networks and rehab clinics across Germany for our 2026 validation cohort — plus access to Horusfit’s LATAM operations for the regional pilot.

Seed round

€1.5M for 18-month runway — to close CE registration, complete the validation study, and launch the enterprise sales motion.
